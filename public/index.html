      Our Story

      About Delhi 6

      Born from love for Old Delhi, built to share it with the world.

      Who We Are

      Storytellers of the Walled City

      Delhi 6 was founded in 2024 by a passionate group of Old Delhi natives who believed that the true magic of Shahjahanabad deserved to be shared — not just through photographs, but through smell, taste, and conversation.

      We are historians, foodies, architects, and storytellers who grew up in the narrow lanes of Chandni Chowk. We are just getting started — and we can't wait to show you our city.
